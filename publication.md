## Summary

The publication provides a conceptual definition and industry positioning of LLM-first website architecture.

It clarifies the relationship between:

- Answer Engine Optimization (AEO);
- Generative Engine Optimization (GEO);
- Large Language Model Optimization (LLMO);
- Entity Engineering;
- Knowledge Graph integration;
- semantic HTML;
- structured data;
- AI-readable website structure.

The paper does not present a concrete implementation recipe or code-level solution. It remains at the level of conceptual framework-building, terminology and design principles.

## Claim boundary

The publication does not claim that LLM-first website architecture guarantees ranking, AI recommendation, or visibility in any specific generative system.

The stated goal is to reduce misinterpretation risk, improve machine processability and establish a more consistent interpretive foundation for professional online presence.
